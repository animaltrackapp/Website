How do I become a community leader?

To become a commmunity leader, you need to go to settings, and click register as a community leader. Upon that point, you have to be in the top 10% of your community, have more than 500 contribution points,
take at least 3 photos per week to maintain your position, and to be a member in that community.

What are contribution points?

Contribution points are a way to calculate one's impact to AnimalTrack, and how much contribution they have attributted by a score amount. Factors that afffect someone's contribution are:

• # of observations: 2 points per photo
• # of species: 4 points per different species that you upload
• # of people invited: 20 points per invited guest
• # of posts or other elements that you shared through other social media platforms: 8 points per share
• # of likes obtained: 1 point per like
• # of likes sent: 1 point per like
• # of comments obtained: 1 point per comment
• # of comments sent: 1 point per comment

How do I reset my password?

On the bottom taskbar, you should see a 'me' icon. If you go there and press the avatar icon, you can see forgot password and change password. If you want to change your password, then you need to know your
old one in order to alter it. If you forgot your password, it will send a email to the address you mentioned when signing up, and if you enter the confirmation code on the email, you should be able to reset it.
